UM, WHAT?

Ever wanted to seek revenge on those suppressed feelings from middle and high school? Then this is the game for you! Play as your favorite t(w)een show icon and brawl out all of those toxic moments. Punch and block your way into solace and retrospective recognition. Wrestle away all those bullies, and come out on top of the pack and keep Nintendo (and all other offenders) quakking in its boots.

CHARACTERS

With an assortment of characters to choose from, engross yourself in those fond memories of middle school angst and high school drama. Pick your fighter carefully, because they all have the same stats and are built using the same model:

True Jackson VP

If she can run an office, she can run the world. Play as True Jackson and show all those opponents how to post up professionally.

iCarly

With a camera attached to her hip, iCarly is ready to film and profit off her fighting skills. Play as iCarly to keep up her reputation and make it big online.

Zoe 101

With her friends cheering on in the background, play as Zoe as she shows the world that a previously all-bois school has nothing on her.

Hannah Montana

The best of both worlds? How about the best at the game! Play as Hannah Montana and show them that, "everybody makes mistakes" but they won't slide.
